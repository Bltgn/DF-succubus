# Known issues with other mods and how to fix those #

Here are the issues related to other mods and how to fix that.

## Accelerated DF ##
You will have no clothes or anvils.

* Open entity_fooccubus.txt and add the following lines at the end :
[PERMITTED_REACTION:PIG_IRON_MAKING]
[PERMITTED_REACTION:STEEL_MAKING]
[PANTS:ITEM_PANTS_PANTS:COMMON]
[SHOES:ITEM_SHOES_SHOES:COMMON]
[ARMOR:ITEM_ARMOR_SHIRT:COMMON]
