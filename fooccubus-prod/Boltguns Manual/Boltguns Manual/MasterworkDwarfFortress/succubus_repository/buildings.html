Category Titles

All workshops are seperated into different categories in the build menu. These category titles can also be build, they will spell out their name. This can be used to label areas ingame, which is great for succession forts. They cost no buildmaterials.

TANNING: Adds specific tanning reactions for each animal skin type. You can tan skin, tough skin, chitin, shell and scales into an amount of leather based on the creatures size. Usually around 2-4 pieces, smaller creatures might give none, while massive monsters give dozens.

LEATHER: Normal leather can be used for suede(3) or vellum(3) from one piece. Suede is very thin leather, useless for combat, but good for backpacks or quivers. Vellum is used for the library system.

FUR/STOMACH/GUT/HORN: Tan stomachs directly into a waterskins, rope from guts/intestines and pelts into a furs. A fur is the very same as leather, but also protects from cold. Fur is needed for glacier embarks. If you dont want the fur, you can shave of the hairs to create a piece of leather and a unit of hair thread. Horns can be shaped into a small dagger, for eariest militias.

FEATHERS: Birds leave feathers upon being butchered.

Material strength:

Leather = Vanilla Leather. ::: Tough leather = Copper.

Studded leather = Bronze. ::: Lamellar leather = Iron.

Chitinplate = Bronze. ::: Rigid Chitin = Iron, very light.

Scaleplate = Bronze. ::: Rigid Scale = Iron, fireproof.

Shellplate = Bronze. ::: Rigid Shellplate = Between iron and steel, heavy.

Slaughterhouse -

Build labor
Skill
Buildmat
Dimension
 Requirements
Architect
Beast Dissection
4 Blocks, 2 Mechanisms, 1 Saw Blade
5x3
Mason & Mechanic & Forge

Every megabeast will leave a special corpse in this mod and can be butchered in the slaughterhouse to receive 3 items. A trophy that can be kept in a display case or traded away, a special reward that is specific to the creature, and a megabeast soul. The soul can be used to revive the beast in the Necromantic Altar. Keep in mind that titans and forgotten beasts are not megabeasts and cant be affected by modding.

Megabeasts also give 60 meat, or 50 boulders, or 25 metal bars upon being butchered, depending on the bodytype of the beast. You will always get 1 heart.

Example: Kill dragon, butcher dragon corpse, get 10 straps of dragonscale. A steel quality, fireproof leather type. You will also get the dragon skull, worth a lot, and the soul.

Stonecrafting & Pottery
2 Blocks
5x3
Mason or Carpenter

The Pottery shapes and dries clay items. You can make clay, fire clay and kaolinite items from the collected raw materials, resulting in greenware items. Greenware is a special name for wet clay. The workshop will automatically queue two drying reactions, from greenware to leather-hard, from leather-hard to bone-dry. The items can not be used until they are fired in the Clay Oven.

To make it simpler: Shape Item => Dry => Dry => Fire Item.

The material progression is: Clay => Greenware => Leather-Hard => Bone-Dry => Ceramic

Each reaction has a 5% chance to fail, simulating impurities in the clay.

 Each kind of tree in the mod can be planted in farm plots, if you are in the correct biome and have the acorns or spores. Trees, Ironbark, Steeloak, Fungiwood, Sporetrees, Crystal Wood, Glassthorns, Nethercaps, Shadethorn, Feather and Glumprong can be farmed. Seeds must be brought at embark, bought from caravans, or discovered from cutting collected tree saplings.

All trees grow all year and need at least one year to mature. The tree saplings are treated as plants and stored as such. Only the Sawmill can cut them into wood logs.

The Archeologist uses found fossils, relics and treasures to create random rewards. Fossils are single tiles in rock. Ruined brick veins have a higher chance of containing any type of relics and treasure.

Fossils actually count as gems, but please do not cut them in the Jeweller, it makes them unusable to the Archeologist. All reactions are run automatically, your Archeologist will pick up the fossil and choose the right reaction for you.

TEMPLES

Temples provides many upgrades and rituals for your succubi, providing upgrade or affecting the site as a whole. Many of those jobs requires souls that are harvested from dead creatures (see the well of souls below).

Rituals sharing a similar goals are attached to a theme:
  • Lust: Increase attributes and drop enemies abilities.
  • Wrath: Increase a warrior's damage, or directly hit enemies.
  • Pride: Raises the fort's happiness.
  • Greed: Increases prductivity.
  • Sloth: Upgrade pets.
  • Gluttony: Consume ressources to heal or boost the succubi.
  • Envy: Take control over invaders or the entire site.
  • Fire: Burn enemies, causes a lot of damage everywhere.
Those rituals also may include side effects including irritability, likeness for combat, personnality alterations, increase in body fat, sudden thirst, learning of skills, unwanted attention, spontaneaous wildfires and other funny moments.

The temple of sin provides upgrades to your succubi. You can use the labor manager (shift + P) to decide who will come and receive it.

  • Reinforce Wings (lust): Provide the ability to fly to one succubus. Please note that flight in fort mode is untested at best and skyborn succubi must have a walkable path to their target before considering taking off.
  • Learn martial excitation (lust): Increases combat abilities based on happiness. Armok may have mercy of your enemies if they meet an ecstatic succubus.
  • Learn the secret of lust: The succubus becomes a courtesan and receive access to several interactions that affect intelligent creatures. Her enemies will have a trouble raising a sword at her. At regular intervals she will become lusty and will multiply both her and other succubi's speed.
  • Learn berserk rage (wrath): Increases combat abilities based on unhappiness. It will still provide a small boost if the succubus is happy. If she is miserable however...
  • Devour soul (glutonny): Eat a soul to receive a slight increase in physical abilities.
  • Learn abyssal gaze (lust): Render an enemy unable to think straight, they may even fall inconscious after a while.
  • Learn necrosis chant (wrath): An enemy is hit by nausea and necrosis. This interaction has a long delay.

Here you can unleash spells that can affect the entire site.

  • Forget death (pride): Causes the succubi to forget recent tragedies. The number of bad thoughts removed is linked to the number of friends the worker made.

  • Protective tentacles (sloth): Tentacle monsters will increase the defense of nearby succubi for two months.

The abyssal temple allow you to reach for the fools who dare to attack your fort and entice them to perform unwise actions. Failing that, you can also bind spells on them from the confort of your dungeon.

  • Lure invaders inside (envy): Siegers will break camp and all run into your fort. This is useful if your defenses are ready and you do not wish to wait further.

The altar of nightmares allow the succubi to visit the mortals in their dreams and whisper them new desires. This can entice leaders into making unwise decisions this way or lead soldiers and crafters into revealing their secrets.

  • Lure caravan: Call down a caravan from an hostile civ for you to butcher and loot.
